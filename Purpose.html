Purpose: This HTML file is designed to present the concept of a Blockchain-Based Credit Scoring platform for unbanked populations. Key Sections: Concept Overview: Describes the idea of a decentralized credit scoring system using alternative financial data (e.g., utility bills, rent payments) to generate credit scores for individuals without a banking history. Innovative Twist: Tech Enhancement: Highlights the integration of AI to analyze non-traditional financial data, improving existing credit scoring algorithms.
